I went to London in 2013. I studied for my master degree of Human-Computer Interaction at University College London.

With my software engineering experience and HCI knowledge, I explored a lot of design possibilities and got inspired on how human interacts with machine and human interacts with each other.

I went back to Beijing and joined ThoughtWorks in 2015.

In the year of 2015, I travelled to Chengdu, Shenzhen, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Xi’an, and Haikou. Working and travelling across China created many opportunities for me to see how experience design changed people’s online and offline behaviours and vice versa.

I’m sharing with you few of projects and initiatives I have been working on recently.
